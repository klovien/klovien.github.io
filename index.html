Hi, there!

My name is Peng Li. I hold a Ph.D. degree from the Universitat Pompeu Fabra (Spain) . I am currently working as a post-doc fellow at the University of Oslo (Norway).

My research focuses on the second language pronunciation, especially the effects of hand gestures in learning L2 phonology. I also investigate L2 speech production with focuses on (a) how Chinese students produce an L2 and (b) how L2 learners produce Chinese.

In Chinese studies, I am interested in the prosody-pragmatic interface and lexical exchange between China and Japan.

Feel free to contact me when you have any questions or comments about my research, or if you are interested in collaboration!
